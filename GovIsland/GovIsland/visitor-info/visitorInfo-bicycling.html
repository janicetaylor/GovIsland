Visitor Info: Bicycling
Visitors are permitted to bring their own bicycles onto the ferries to Governors Island. The circumference of the island is 2.2 miles of car-free cycling, all of it along the water. There are numerous paths and roads that crisscross the island and pass near the historic buildings, Fort Jay and Castle Williams. Bicyclists are not permitted to ride across the former parade grounds or Nolan Park.

Bike Rentals

Bike rentals are available on the island Friday-Sunday. The rentals are managed by Bike and Roll, a private concession. On Friday afternoons, the last rental is at 3:30 and all bikes must be returned by 4:30. On Saturdays and Sundays, the last rental is at 4:30 and all bikes must be returned by 5:30. The pickup/dropoff locations are near the Post Hospital and Building 140.

Fridays are Bike and Roll's Free Bike Friday. Visitors can rent a bike for free for up to one hour. Saturdays and Sundays, rental costs are the following:
• Comfort Bicycles: $15 for 2 hours, $25 all day
• Children's Bicycles: $10 for 2 hours, $20 all day
Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays, rental costs are the following:
• Small QuadCycle: $25 for a half hour, $35 an hour
• Large QuadCycle: $30 for a half hour, $40 an hour
• Tandem Bikes: $25 for 2 hours, $45 all day

All rentals include a helmet free of charge. Anyone under 18 years old is required to wear a helmet. Kids equipment only for children one year old and up. A valid photo ID (U.S. state issued driver's license or passport) is required as a deposit, to be held for the duration of the ride, for each adult rental. For group rentals, contact Bike and Roll directly at (212) 260-0400.
