Visitor Info: Driving-Parking
Driving

Driving from the East Side:
Take FDR Drive to Exit 1, South Ferry. As you exit, stay in the left lane and turn left around the (left) U-turn at the end of the street, in front of the Staten Island Ferry terminal. The Battery Maritime Building is the historic ferry terminal just to the northeast of the Staten Island Ferry terminal.

Driving from the West Side:
Take the West Side Highway around the tip of Lower Manhattan and go through the Battery Underpass. When you emerge from the underpass, take the first exit, Exit 1, Staten Island Ferry. Stay left and make a left-hand U-turn onto South Street going south. Stay to your left and turn left around the (left) U-turn at the end of the street, in front of the Staten Island Ferry terminal. The Battery Maritime Building is the historic ferry terminal just to the northeast of the Staten Island Ferry terminal.

Driving on Local Streets:
Take Broadway south onto Whitehall Street. Turn left onto Water Street. Go one block and turn right onto Broad Street. Go one block and turn right onto South Street. Merge into the left lane and make the (left) U-turn at the end of the street in front of the Staten Island Ferry terminal. The Battery Maritime Building is the historic ferry terminal just to the northeast of the Staten Island Ferry terminal.

Parking

There is no public parking at the Battery Maritime Building and cars are not allowed on Governors Island. The closest parking garages to the ferry are:
• Quik Park, 1 New York Plaza
• Icon Parking Systems, 14-26 South William Street
• Impark, 55 Water St
• State Pearl Garage, 1 Battery Park Plaza #2
• Ocean Car Park, 17 Battery Place
