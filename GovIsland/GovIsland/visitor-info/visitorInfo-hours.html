Visitor Info: Operating Hours
Governors Island will re-open to the public on Friday, May 27, 2011, and will be open every Friday, Saturday and Sunday through September 25. The island is open from 10 AM to 5 PM on Fridays and 10 AM to 7 PM on Saturday and Sundays.

For the first time, the Island will also be open on holiday Mondays (Memorial Day, July 4th and Labor Day). The island is closed to the public at all other times, and is only open to those on official business with the Trust for Governors Island and the National Park Service, or attending the Urban Assembly New York Harbor School.

For concertgoers, read the Concertgoers Info section.
