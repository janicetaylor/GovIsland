Visitor Info: Subway
Downtown construction frequently disrupts subway service. Before your trip, be sure to check subway status on mta.info and on Twitter @NYCTSubwayScoop. MTA Travel Info dial 718-330-1234. New York State Travel and Transit Dial 511.

Subways to the Battery Maritime Building located at 10 South Street: The two closest stations are the 1 (South Ferry Station) and R (Whitehall Street Station). The two stations were connected together recently, and both share an exit directly across from the Staten Island Ferry terminal, next door to the Battery Maritime Building. However, only the South Ferry Station is accessible with an elevator to the street for wheelchairs, strollers and bicycles. Whitehall Street Station is not ADA compliant.

Subway Service:

1 Train
South Ferry Station
South Street & Whitehall Street
The station is near Battery Park and the Battery Maritime Building.

R Train
Whitehall Street Station
South Street & Whitehall Street
The station is directly across the street from the Battery Maritime Building located at 10 South Street, adjacent to the Staten Island Ferry. No elevator and not ADA compliant.

4 or 5 Train
Bowling Green Station
Battery Place & Broadway
The station is a 5-minute walk to the ferry. Exit at the platform at the front of the train. Walk south on State Street three blocks to Battery Park. Look for the Staten Island ferry terminal, and the Battery Maritime Building is located next to it, on the north side. Two elevators, it is ADA compliant.
