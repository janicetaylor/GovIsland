Visitor Info: Bus
Three New York City bus lines pick up and drop off passengers in front of the Staten Island Ferry terminal, next door to the Battery Maritime Building ferry.

M5: 5th/6th Avenues/Riverside Drive
The M5 operates between South Ferry and West 178th Street/Broadway in Washington Heights every day. Limited-Stop service is provided weekdays; the Limited-Stop areas are between 157th and 135th Sts on Broadway and between 72nd Street and 8th or 14th Streets. Operates between South Ferry and West 178th Street/Broadway (George Washington Bridge Bus Station), daily.

M15: 1st/2nd Avenues
The M15 operates between South Ferry and 2 Av/East 126th St. On weekdays some buses operate between Pike St/Henry Street and 2 Av/East 126th St. Select Bus Service is provided every day. Local service operates between South Ferry and 2 Av/East 126th Street, daily.

M15 (Select Bus Service): 1st/2nd Avenues
Select Bus Service operates between South Ferry and 2 Av/East 126th St. Buses stop only at special stops along the M15 route. Fare is paid before boarding using a machine at the stop. There is a fare collection box installed at South Ferry.

M20: 7th/8th Avenues/Hudson Street
Operates between South Ferry and West 63rd Street/Broadway (Lincoln Center), daily.
